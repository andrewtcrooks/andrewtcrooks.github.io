About the Founder

Andrew T. Crooks

Founder of DataNova Consulting LLC

Hi, I'm Andrew. I build software products and deploy self-hosted infrastructure for businesses that want to own their tools instead of renting them.

With a background in data science and astrophysics, I approach every project with an engineer's mindset: understand the problem deeply, build the simplest solution that works, and make sure it keeps working.

DataNova started because I kept seeing the same pattern: businesses paying monthly fees for cloud services they could own outright, or spending hours on manual tasks that should be automated. I decided to do something about it.

  • B.S., Physics (Astrophysics focus) – University of California San Diego, 2011

Technical Expertise

  • AI/ML Integration – OpenAI APIs, local LLMs, speech-to-text, OCR
  • Desktop Applications – Electron, Python, cross-platform development
  • Monitoring & Analytics – Uptime Kuma, Umami, Nginx Proxy Manager

My Approach

As a veteran, I bring discipline and clarity to everything I build. I don't believe in over-engineering or unnecessary complexity. If a simple solution works, that's what you get.

I use the same tools I deploy for clients. Paperless manages my documents. n8n automates my workflows. Uptime Kuma monitors my infrastructure. I'm not selling you something I wouldn't use myself.
