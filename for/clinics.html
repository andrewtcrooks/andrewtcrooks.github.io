What we automate for practices

New Patient Intake

Form submission → EHR entry → welcome email → appointment confirmation

A patient fills out your intake form. Everything else happens automatically: data in your system, welcome email sent, appointment confirmed. No manual data entry.

Appointment Reminders

Booking created → reminder at 48h → reminder at 2h

Scheduled texts or emails at 48 hours and 2 hours before each appointment. No manual sends. Fewer no-shows. No one has to remember.

Insurance & Billing Follow-Up

Claim status → no change in X days → follow-up triggered

When a claim hasn't moved in a defined window, an automated follow-up is sent or a staff alert is triggered. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Post-Visit Tasks

Appointment marked complete → follow-up tasks triggered

After an appointment closes: send patient instructions, request a review, schedule follow-up, or trigger billing. No one has to manually kick it off.
