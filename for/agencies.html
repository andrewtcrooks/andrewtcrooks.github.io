What we automate for agencies

Client Onboarding

Contract signed → project created → tools provisioned → kickoff meeting booked

A new client signs and within minutes: their project is created, tools are set up, the kickoff meeting link is sent, and your team gets notified. Identical every time.

Automated Reporting

Scheduled trigger → pull from 4 sources → format report → send to client

Pull data from Google Analytics, Ads, social platforms, and your PM tool. Format it. Send it on schedule. No copy-paste, no missed sends.

Lead Follow-Up

New inquiry → immediate reply → follow-up sequence → sales alert

Every inbound lead gets an immediate acknowledgment. If no response in 48 hours, a follow-up goes out. Your sales team only sees leads that haven't responded.

Invoice Triggers

Milestone hit → invoice drafted → PM notified → sent on approval

When a project hits a defined milestone, an invoice is drafted in your billing tool and your PM is notified for review — before it goes to the client.
