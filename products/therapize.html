Frequently Asked Questions

Is Therapize HIPAA compliant?

Therapize is designed with HIPAA in mind. All processing happens locally on your device - audio, transcripts, and AI analysis never leave your computer. No cloud storage, no third-party data sharing.

Does it work offline?

Yes! After initial setup and model download, Therapize works completely offline. Perfect for private practices with strict data policies.

What audio sources does it support?

Therapize captures from your computer's microphone input. This works with in-person sessions, Zoom calls, telehealth platforms - any audio source your microphone can pick up.

Can I edit the generated notes?

Absolutely. Therapize generates a draft for your review. You can edit, adjust, and approve before exporting. You're always in control of the final documentation.

What happens to my data?

Your data stays on your computer. Keep recordings for review or delete them when you're done. Nothing is ever uploaded anywhere.
