Before You Reach Out

What happens on the triage call?

We ask you to walk us through the workflow that's costing you the most time. We confirm whether automation is the right fix, and if so, give you a rough idea of scope and cost. No sales pressure.

Is the triage call free?

Yes. No cost, no obligation. If we can't help, we'll tell you that on the call.

What should I prepare?

Just know which repetitive task is costing you the most time or causing the most errors. You don't need to know anything technical.

Do you work with businesses outside the US?

Yes. All sprints are done remotely. If your tools are accessible online, we can automate them.
