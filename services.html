Is this a fit?

Good fit

  • You have at least one admin task someone does manually 5+ times a week
  • You use common business tools — Gmail, Slack, Airtable, HubSpot, QuickBooks, Calendly, Stripe, Typeform, or similar
  • You want a working system, not just a consultation or a recommendations document
  • You're a business owner or ops manager who can make the call
  • You can commit 2–3 hours across the week for calls and review

Not a fit (yet)

  • You need custom software development built from scratch
  • You're looking for IT helpdesk or general tech support
  • You want to evaluate 10 different tools before any decision is made
  • You don't have a clear pain point — just a vague feeling you should "do automation"
  • You need enterprise-scale infrastructure (100+ employees)

Common Questions

Do I need to buy new software?

Usually no. We connect the tools you already use. If a new tool would meaningfully improve the outcome, we'll tell you upfront — before any commitment.

How technical do I need to be?

Not at all. You describe the problem in plain terms. We handle the implementation. Handoffs include non-technical SOPs your whole team can follow.

What if it doesn't work the way I expected?

The triage call and scoping process is designed to catch that before we start. Scope is agreed in writing before kickoff. No scope surprises.

What tools can you automate?

We specialize in n8n, which connects 400+ apps natively — Gmail, Slack, Airtable, HubSpot, Calendly, QuickBooks, Stripe, Typeform, and more. If you use it, we can likely automate it.
