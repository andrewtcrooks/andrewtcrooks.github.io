If the scope is tight, yes. The triage call is specifically designed to find the one bottleneck with the highest return. We won't take on a sprint we can't deliver in 7 days.

What if I have more than one broken workflow?

Start here. Clients who complete a sprint and see results typically continue with an Ops Automation Buildout. One win at a time is the right approach.

We specialize in n8n, which connects 400+ apps natively — Gmail, Slack, Airtable, HubSpot, Calendly, QuickBooks, Stripe, Typeform, and more. If you use it, we can probably automate it.

We confirm scope before we start. If something is out of scope, we tell you on Day 1 — not Day 7. Scope is agreed in writing before kickoff.

Do I need my own server or infrastructure?

Not usually. Most Sprint automations run in the cloud or connect existing SaaS tools. If infrastructure is needed, we'll discuss options on the triage call.

What happens after the 2 weeks of support?

You own everything we built. If you want ongoing support, we offer Monthly Automation Ops. If something breaks outside the support window, hourly support is available.
